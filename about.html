InsideTheSign is a boutique design and signage studio run by an artist who knows the industry from the inside.

Linnea Maas brings a trained visual artist's eye, years of experience as both designer and commercial signage project manager, and deep expertise across the full range of commercial signage.

She develops each project from concept to completion, managing everything required to get it fabricated, permitted, and installed.

Linnea speaks fluently to every person on a project for a seamless, efficient, and enjoyable process. Whether you arrive with a complete vision or need help developing one from scratch, you'll have one point of contact from the first conversation through final installation.

Linnea works with a curated network of local Minneapolis fabricators and installers. Clients get a personal studio's attention and a professional operation's capability, concept to installation.

Areas of expertise
  • Exterior building signage
  • Channel letters
  • Monument signs
  • Interior environments
  • ADA and wayfinding
  • Murals
  • Environmental graphics
  • Art forward decor
  • Placemaking
  • Vehicle and trailer wraps
