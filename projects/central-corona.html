Central Corona

TypeMural
Year2024
LocationMinneapolis, MN

A playful, character-driven mural commissioned by Corona, built around a cast of illustrated figures in bold, saturated color. The scene spans the wall as a continuous composition, designed to be cheerful and immersive and to give the space a personality people want to share.

The style is loose and expressive, with the energy of work made specifically for its wall and its room. A mural that turns a surface into a reason to come back.
